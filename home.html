Team one's World

About

Team one's world is a html based game.

This game simulates the daily life of Multimedia Nusantara University College student

How To Play

1. Choose your avatar

2. Enter your name in the text box then click the Gaskan button

3. Click next page button to enter the game

4. Look at the progress bar, your job is to make sure the bar is filled all the time If its empty, there will be warning and u might lose the game.

5. To fill the bar, all you need to do is click the buttons below the progress bars.

6. To win the game, you must reach the 8th semester by filling the study bar

7. Thats how to play Team one's world, Enjoy :)
